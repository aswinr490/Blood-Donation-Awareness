Why Blood Donation is Important

Blood donation is a vital part of healthcare systems worldwide. Each year, millions of people require blood for surgeries, cancer treatment, trauma care, and other medical conditions. The blood donated by individuals saves lives and helps maintain a steady supply for patients in need.

  • It promotes the production of new blood cells in the body.
